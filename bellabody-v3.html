Our Story

Born in Toronto.
Built for recovery.

In 2016, Stephanie Flores saw what was missing in Canada — expert, compassionate care for people recovering from aesthetic surgeries. She built Canada's first fully licensed postoperative recovery facility from the ground up, right here in the GTA.

From Manual Lymphatic Drainage to wood therapy, body contouring to laser treatments — BellaBody's certified specialists deliver clinical results in an environment as polished as the outcome you're working toward.

Today we operate two locations in Mississauga and Vaughan, serving clients from across Canada who trust their recovery to no one else.
